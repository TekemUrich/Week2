About Us

Welcome to Abel Medical Clinic, a Private GP Medical Centre with Head office in London and locations in Birmingham and Swindon. Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable healthcare services to our patients, delivered by a team of experienced and well trained medical professionals.

Our Services

Mental Health

Welcome to Abel Medical Clinic, where we specialise in providing leading mental health treatment in Birmingham. Your mental health is a crucial aspect of your overall well-being, and we are dedicated to helping you achieve positive mental wellness. If you are facing mental health challenges, you are not alone, and we are here to support you every step of the way.

Discover comprehensive mental health support at our clinic, where our team of psychiatrists, psychologists, therapists, and neurodiversity experts collaborates to provide personalised care. We focus on understanding your symptoms and offer holistic management approaches for your mental well-being

Blood Tests
At times, you might feel the need for a complete health screen, regardless of whether or not you are experiencing any uncomfortable symptoms. If you are over a certain age or belong to a high-risk group, your GP may recommend that you undergo regular check-ups. Abel Medical Clinic offers various health check packages to cater to a diverse range of needs. We offer tailored check-ups for men and women to assess conditions such as menopause, infertility, liver function, blood tests, diabetes tests, cholesterol testing, asthma testing and many others. Our packages include a full face-to-face consultation with ourdoctor who will provide you with healthcare advice and be able to refer you to a specialist, if required.

Imaging (X-Rays and Scans)
At Abel Medical Clinic, we offer a seamless approach to healthcare, encompassing not only expert medical consultations but also cutting-edge diagnostic services. After an initial GP consultation, our skilled doctors may recommend further investigations to gain a comprehensive understanding of your health. We provide state-of-the-art X-rays and scans, enabling precise and timely diagnoses. These imaging services play a crucial role in identifying various conditions, ensuring accurate assessments, and facilitating effective treatment plans. Rest assured, your health and well-being are our top priorities, and we are dedicated to providing you with the best possible care.
